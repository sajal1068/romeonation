Earnings Distribution Model

The earnings distribution model ensures fair compensation for all participants while promoting sustainability.

Direct Referral Bonus

5%

Earned when a member refers another who invests. For example, if a new member invested with $100, then the referrer will receives $5 as a direct referral income.

Daily Profit Return

0.5%

You will receive 0.5% of your invested amount daily as a daily return income (ROI) .

Level Bonus

4% to 1%

Distributed across 4 levels of referrals, rewarding deeper engagement and multi-tiered recruitment. This bonus you will earn on your downline investment. Whenever they will invest you will receive the level bonus instatntly.

Leadership Bonus

0.25% to 15%

Distributed across 50 levels of referrals. This bonus you will earn on your downline daily return income (ROI). Whenever they will get the roi income you will receive the leadership bonus from their daily return income.

Withdrawal & Recycling Mechanism

Withdrawal and Recycling Mechanism typically refers to the process of withdrawing capital or profits from accounts and then reusing or reinvesting those funds back into the same account.

Withdrawal Policy

Members can withdraw their earnings once they accumulate at least $20. This encourages active participation and engagement with the platform.

Recycling Mechanism

After reaching the 200% or 500% income threshold of invested amount, after that the earning will put on hold and account will marked as inactive account. Users need to reinvest again. This "recycling" process is crucial for maintaining the platform's longevity and ensuring continuous community participation.
